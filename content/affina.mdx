# Affina

Before this transformation, Affina was growing fast —  
but the brand, product, and marketing visuals were not yet aligned.

The core question that shaped this project:

## “What should a financial protection platform *feel* like?”

My role was not just to redesign screens.  
I rebuilt how Affina **looks, behaves, and scales** across every touchpoint.

---

# Scope of Responsibility

• End-to-end brand rebranding  
• Brand guidelines creation  
• Full asset ecosystem design  
• Website theme redesign  
• Product UI alignment (Affina 2.0, Pro, Dashboard, Portal)  
• Design System & Component Library  
• AI-enhanced design workflow implementation  

---

# Phase 1 — Brand Rebuild

Affina needed a clearer personality:  
**modern, trustworthy, minimal — yet human.**

## Brand Foundations

• Refined visual direction  
• Color system & semantic palette  
• Typography hierarchy  
• Layout & spacing principles  
• Illustration & imagery direction

## Brand Guidelines

I built a structured and scalable brand guideline system covering:

• Logo usage & protection rules  
• Color combinations & accessibility ratios  
• Typography scale & usage  
• Grid & layout framework  
• Social & marketing applications  

This became the **single source of truth** for Product, Marketing, and Media teams.

# Phase 2 — Asset Ecosystem

Rebranding required scale, not just aesthetics.

I developed a reusable asset library including:

• Key visuals  
• Daily social post templates  
• Paid ads creatives  
• Event & partnership materials  
• Sales & internal presentation decks  

All assets followed the same tokenized visual logic to ensure consistency and faster production.

# Phase 3 — Website Theme Transformation

The previous website lacked hierarchy and emotional clarity.

## Before

• Inconsistent visual language  
• Weak section hierarchy  
• Overloaded financial information  
• Low narrative clarity  

## After

• Clear storytelling structure  
• Improved information hierarchy  
• Stronger whitespace rhythm  
• Unified visual system aligned with brand  
• Clearer conversion flow  

The objective:  
Make financial protection feel simple and trustworthy — not overwhelming.

# Phase 4 — Product UI Alignment

The new brand needed to live inside the product ecosystem.

I worked closely with BA and Product Owner to:
