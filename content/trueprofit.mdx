## About  

At TrueProfit, I took on two key responsibilities:  

1. **Support in designing the landing page** for [trueprofit.io](https://trueprofit.io/), aimed at marketing and user acquisition.  
2. **Design onboarding illustrations** for the in-app experience, ensuring a friendly and coherent visual style aligned with the product's identity.  

---

## Landing Page Design  

The goal was to create a homepage that clearly communicates the product’s value while aligning with the marketing strategy.  

### Process  
- **Understanding requirements**: Reviewed the existing design system and collected expectations from stakeholders. *(~1 week)*  
- **Research & benchmarking**: Studied competitor websites to identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities. *(~1 week)*  
- **Wireframing**: Created low-fidelity homepage layouts based on hierarchy and content structure. *(~2 weeks)*  
- **Information Architecture**: Defined sitemap and content flow for clarity. *(~1 week)*  
- **High-fidelity design**: Applied brand system, refined visual details, and integrated illustration concepts. *(~2–3 weeks)*  
- **Iteration & feedback**: Conducted review sessions with the marketing team to finalize.  

---

## Onboarding Illustration  

### Process  
- **Analyze onboarding flow**: Identified key steps where illustrations would support user understanding. *(~1 week)*  
- **Concept sketching**: Drafted multiple illustration directions consistent with the brand’s visual identity. *(~1 week)*  
- **Final illustration set**: Produced vector-based onboarding illustrations for smooth integration into the product. *(~2 weeks)*
